How LLMs Work

The Library Analogy

Imagine reading every book, article, and documentation set ever published online.

Someone starts a sentence.

You complete it — based on everything you've read.

That's roughly what an LLM does.

  • The "library" = a significant portion of the public internet
  • The "reading" = training (happened before you use it)
  • The "completion" = generated word by word, every time

What AI Does Well

  • ✅ Recognizing patterns ("does this doc follow the structure?")
  • ✅ Generating text in a given style
  • ✅ Checking completeness against a checklist
  • ✅ Reformatting and summarizing
  • ✅ Applying style rules consistently

AI is excellent at the form of documentation.
